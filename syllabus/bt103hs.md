<div class="syllabus-section">
  <h2>Course Objective</h2>
  <p>The aim of this course is to provide sound knowledge of differential and integral calculus.</p>
</div>

<div class="syllabus-section">
  <h2>Course Contents</h2>
  
  <div class="unit">
    <h3>Unit 1: Derivatives and its Applications <span class="unit-hours">[20 Hrs]</span></h3>
    <ul>
      <li><a href="/syllabus/bt103hs/limit-continuity-differentiability/">1.1 General concept of limit, continuity and differentiability of a function with some examples.</a></li>
      <li><a href="/syllabus/bt103hs/tangent-normal/">1.2 Equation of tangent and normal to the curve.</a></li>
      <li><a href="/syllabus/bt103hs/successive-derivatives/">1.3 Successive derivatives and application of Leibnitz’s theorem.</a></li>
      <li><a href="/syllabus/bt103hs/extrema-functions/">1.4 Extrema of functions of two or three variables.</a></li>
      <li><a href="/syllabus/bt103hs/maclaurin-taylor-expansion/">1.5 Evaluate Maclaurin’s and Taylor’s expansion for variety of functions and use them to solve further advanced problems.</a></li>
      <li><a href="/syllabus/bt103hs/indeterminant-form/">1.6 Indeterminant form and limits (Application of L.Hospital’s rule).</a></li>
      <li><a href="/syllabus/bt103hs/curvature/">1.7 Curvature (Radius of curvature and chord of curvature).</a></li>
      <li><a href="/syllabus/bt103hs/partial-differentiation/">1.8 Partial differentiation of functions, Euler’s theorem.</a></li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="unit">
    <h3>Unit 2: Integral and its Applications <span class="unit-hours">[20 Hrs]</span></h3>
    <ul>
      <li><a href="/syllabus/bt103hs/indefinite-definite-integral/">2.1 Indefinite integral and Definite integral (Properties of definite integral).</a></li>
      <li><a href="/syllabus/bt103hs/improper-integral/">2.2 Improper integral.</a></li>
      <li><a href="/syllabus/bt103hs/reduction-formulae/">2.2 Reduction formulae and Beta and Gamma functions.</a></li>
      <li><a href="/syllabus/bt103hs/area-length-volume/">2.3 Area, length, volume, and surface area (Cartesian form only).</a></li>
      <li><a href="/syllabus/bt103hs/multiple-integral/">2.4 Multiple integral (Double and Triple integral).</a></li>
      <li><a href="/syllabus/bt103hs/change-order-integration/">2.5 Change of order of integration in double integral.</a></li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="unit">
    <h3>Unit 3: Solving first order and first degree differential equations <span class="unit-hours">[5 Hrs]</span></h3>
    <ul>
      <li><a href="/syllabus/bt103hs/differential-equations/">3.1 Variable separable method, linear differential eqation, Bernoulli’s and exact differential equations and its applications.</a></li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>
