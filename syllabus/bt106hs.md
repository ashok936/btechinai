<div class="syllabus-section">
  <h2>Course Objective</h2>
  <p>The course aims at providing the basic knowledge on the concept of sociology and professional ethics. Its main purpose is to make students understand the link among sociological, cultural, economic, political aspects and technology and enable students apply technology to make better society.</p>
</div>
