<div class="syllabus-section">
  <h2>Course Objective</h2>
  <p>The basic objective of the course is to develop linguistic and communicative competence of students with special emphasis on skill development and English language skill for effective presentation.</p>
</div>

<div class="syllabus-section">
  <h2>Course Contents</h2>
  
  <div class="unit">
    <h3>Unit 1: Oral Presentation <span class="unit-hours">[10 Hrs]</span></h3>
    <ul>
      <li><a href="/syllabus/bt105hs/introduction-fundamentals/">1.1 Introduction and Fundamentals of effective oral presentation</a> (Facial expression, Gesture, Eye Contact, Space Distancing, Posture)</li>
      <li><a href="/syllabus/bt105hs/communication-types-dimensions/">1.2 Types and Dimensions of communication</a> (Verbal, Nonverbal, Downward, Upward, Horizontal)</li>
      <li><a href="/syllabus/bt105hs/oral-activities/">1.3 Activities</a> (Group discussion, Debate, Public speaking)</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="unit">
    <h3>Unit 2: Reading <span class="unit-hours">[10 Hrs]</span></h3>
    <ul>
      <li><a href="/syllabus/bt105hs/reading-types-activities/">2.1 Introduction, Types of reading, and Activities</a> (Intensive, Extensive, Skimming, Scanning, Note taking, Summary writing)</li>
      <li><a href="/syllabus/bt105hs/reading-texts/">2.2 Texts for reading</a> (B.P. Koirala, Chinua Achebe, Anton Chekhov, Bonnie Smith Yachel, Bertrand Russell)</li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="unit">
    <h3>Unit 3: Conducting Meeting <span class="unit-hours">[5 Hrs]</span></h3>
    <ul>
      <li><a href="/syllabus/bt105hs/conducting-meeting/">3.1 Introduction, Writing notice, Writing agenda, Writing minutes</a></li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="unit">
    <h3>Unit 4: Writing <span class="unit-hours">[9 Hrs]</span></h3>
    <ul>
      <li><a href="/syllabus/bt105hs/writing-fundamentals/">4.1 Fundamentals of effective writing</a> (Unity, Length, Coherence, Cohesion, Conciseness, Clarity)</li>
      <li><a href="/syllabus/bt105hs/technical-reporting-proposals/">4.2 Technical report and Proposal writing formats</a></li>
      <li><a href="/syllabus/bt105hs/official-letters-memos/">4.3 Writing letters (Official, Job application, Resume) and Memoranda</a></li>
    </ul>
  </div>

  <div class="unit">
    <h3>Unit 5: Elements of Structures and Varieties in English Language <span class="unit-hours">[11 Hrs]</span></h3>
    <ul>
      <li><a href="/syllabus/bt105hs/english-varieties-grammar/">5.1 Varieties in English languages, Preposition; subject-verb arrangement; Voice, reported speech</a></li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>
