<div class="syllabus-section">
  <h2>Course Objectives</h2>
  <p>The main objective of this course is to familiarize students with the basic and conceptual understanding of AI systems and a practical grounding in its concepts.</p>
</div>

<div class="syllabus-section">
  <h2>Laboratory Work</h2>
  <p>There shall be lab exercises on PROLOG/LISP covering the following topics.</p>
  <ol>
    <li>Simple question-answering</li>
    <li>Solving family relation problems</li>
    <li>Numerical problems such as Factorial, Factors, GCD</li>
    <li>Logic-gate programs</li>
    <li>Fundamental search techniques</li>
  </ol>
</div>
