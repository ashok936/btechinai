    <h3>Group A</h3>
    <div class="group-info">
      <span>Answer TWO questions.</span>
      <span>2 × 12 = 24</span>
    </div>

    <div class="question">
      <p><strong>1. (a)</strong> Evaluate $\int x \log x dx$. <span class="marks">[4]</span></p>
      <p><strong>1. (b)</strong> Obtain a reduction formula for $\int \sin^{n} x dx$ and find $\int \sin^{6} x dx$. <span class="marks">[8]</span></p>
    </div>

    <div class="question">
      <p><strong>2. (a)</strong> Tangents are drawn from the origin to the curve $y = \sin x$. Prove that their point of contact lie on $x^{2}y^{2} = x^{2} - y^{2}$. <span class="marks">[4]</span></p>
      <p><strong>2. (b)</strong> If $y = a \cos(\log x) + b \sin(\log x)$, then show that:</p>
      <ul>
        <li>(i) $x^{2}y_{2} + xy_{1} + y = 0$ <span class="marks">[4]</span></li>
        <li>(ii) $x^{2}y_{n+2} + (2n+1)xy_{n+1} + (n^{2}+1)y_{n} = 0$ <span class="marks">[4]</span></li>
      </ul>
    </div>

    <div class="question">
      <p><strong>3. (a)</strong> Evaluate $\iint xy(x+y) dx dy$ over the area between $y = x^{2}$ and $y = x$. <span class="marks">[6]</span></p>
      <p><strong>3. (b)</strong> Solve the equation $x \cos y dy = (xe^{x} \log x + e^{x}) dx$. <span class="marks">[6]</span></p>
    </div>
  </div>

  <div class="group">
    <h3>Group B</h3>
    <div class="group-info">

    <div class="question">
      <p><strong>4.</strong> Use Gamma function to prove: $$\int_{0}^{\pi} \sin^{6} \frac{x}{2} \cos^{8} \frac{x}{2} dx = \frac{5\pi}{2^{11}}$$ <span class="marks">[6]</span></p>
    </div>

    <div class="question">
      <p><strong>5.</strong> Find the area of the asteroid $x^{\frac{2}{3}} + y^{\frac{2}{3}} = a^{\frac{2}{3}}$. <span class="marks">[6]</span></p>
    </div>

    <div class="question">
      <p><strong>6.</strong> Find the volume of the solid formed by revolving the cycloid $x = a(\theta + \sin \theta)$, $y = a(1 + \cos \theta)$. <span class="marks">[6]</span></p>
    </div>

    <div class="question">
      <p><strong>7.</strong> Evaluate: $\lim_{x \to 0} \left(\frac{1}{x^{2}} - \frac{1}{\cos^{2} x}\right)$ <span class="marks">[6]</span></p>
    </div>

    <div class="question">
      <p><strong>8.</strong> Find the asymptotes of the curve: $y^{3} - xy^{2} - x^{2}y + x^{3} + x^{2} - y^{2} = 1$. <span class="marks">[6]</span></p>
    </div>

    <div class="question">
      <p><strong>9.</strong> Find the radius of curvature of the curve $r = a(1 - \cos \theta)$. <span class="marks">[6]</span></p>
    </div>

    <div class="question">
      <p><strong>10.</strong> Solve the equation: $$\frac{dy}{dx} + 2y \tan x = \sin x$$ <span class="marks">[6]</span></p>
