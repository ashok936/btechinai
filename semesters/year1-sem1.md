## Syllabus Details
{: .section-title }

### CMP 101 - Introduction to Computer Programming

**Key Topics:**
- Variables, data types, and operators
- Control flow: loops, conditionals, functions
- Debugging and testing basics
- Mini-project: simple calculator or game

**Study Tips:**
- Focus on logic building before syntax
- Practice 10+ coding problems per week
- Review algorithm complexity basics

---

### MTH 101 - Mathematics I (Calculus)

**Key Topics:**
- Functions and limits
- Derivatives and applications
- Integrals and fundamental theorem
- Series and sequences

**Study Tips:**
- Strong foundation for AI/ML mathematics
- Connect concepts to real-world applications
- Practice derivations and proofs regularly

---

### PHY 101 - Physics I

**Key Topics:**
- Kinematics and dynamics
- Energy and momentum
- Waves and oscillations
- Thermodynamics basics

**Study Tips:**
- Apply physics concepts to computational systems
- Lab reports are 20% of final grade
- Diagrams and visualizations help understanding

---

### ENG 101 - English for Communication

**Key Topics:**
- Technical writing and documentation
- Presentation skills and public speaking
- Reading comprehension
- Business communication

**Study Tips:**
- Write summaries of technical papers
- Practice oral presentations regularly
- Balance clarity with technical accuracy

---

### CSC 101 - Digital Logic

**Key Topics:**
- Boolean algebra and truth tables
- Logic gates: AND, OR, NOT, XOR
- Combinational circuits
- Sequential circuits and flip-flops

**Study Tips:**
- Practice truth table construction
- Understand circuit design fundamentals
- Lab components are practical and graded

---

### MEC 101 - Engineering Drawing

**Key Topics:**
- Orthographic projections
- Isometric and perspective views
- CAD software basics
- Technical drawing standards

**Study Tips:**
- Use CAD tools (AutoCAD/LibreCAD)
- Practice 2D and 3D sketching
- Portfolio of drawings is assessment
